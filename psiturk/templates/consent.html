Informed consent:
        You are being asked to participate in a project being conducted by a research team at Harvard University, under the direction of Dr. Tomer Ullman. We are interested in how people are able to reason quickly and in a commonsense way about the different objects and people they encounter in the world around them. A better understanding of commonsense reasoning has implications for building more intelligent machines that can reason in a more human-like way, lowering risk in different physical situations. During this study, you will be presented with verbal events. To assess your understanding of these displays, you will be asked questions and you will respond by selecting an appropriate response.  

        All information collected from the study will be associated with a randomly generated Prolific code number or Prolific ID, which is the only identifying data you will be requested to provide.
